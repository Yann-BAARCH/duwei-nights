The recording caught almost nothing: air, a low motor somewhere, one brief movement outside the frame.

On speakers it sounded empty. Through headphones it opened a little. The room in the recording and the room I was in began to overlap.

When it ended, the silence after it was different. Not deeper. Just less neutral.
