Past midnight the suggestions got less useful and more interesting. I kept clicking. Rehearsal rooms, bad rips, handheld videos, songs with no names that stayed just long enough to tilt the room.

One band came up with no introduction worth keeping. The camera was too far back. The vocal nearly gave out on the first line. Then it held.

I did not look them up. The song was enough for one night.
