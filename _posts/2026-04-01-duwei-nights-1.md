it started without a clear reason

just a habit, maybe  
late nights, a glass, something golden  
a familiar name in the background

and then the drift

falling into it  
the endless depth of the internet  
before everything became too clean, too optimized

just wandering  
finding things  
losing time

music, mostly

notes, rhythms, fragments of voices  
something beautiful, sometimes sad  
sometimes too intellectual  
sometimes purely emotional

there is love in it  
there is also rejection  
instinct, attraction, repulsion

it’s not always clear  
but it feels real

i don’t always create  
sometimes i don’t even try

but i absorb  
constantly

culture as fuel  
something necessary  
something almost physical

a reaction in the body  
something close to instinct  
something that stays

i like getting lost in it  
a kind of intoxication  
as long as it doesn’t take everything  
even if sometimes it does

this space comes from that

a place to keep traces  
what i find  
what i feel  
what stays

music  
images  
fragments  
ideas  
moments

not a journal  
not exactly

just something simple  
and honest

something i can come back to

and maybe  
something you can get lost in too
